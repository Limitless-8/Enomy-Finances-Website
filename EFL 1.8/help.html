Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Your guide to understanding investments at Enomy Finances

📌 What are the investment plans?

Basic Savings Plan: Low-risk, 1.2%–2.4% return. No tax.

Savings Plan Plus: Moderate return (3%–5.5%). 10% tax on profit above £12,000.

Managed Stock Investments: Higher risk/reward (4%–23%). Taxes apply on profits over £12,000 and £40,000.

📈 How is my return calculated?

Returns are estimated based on monthly contributions, duration, and the plan's interest rate range.

💰 What is the minimum investment?

  • Basic: £50/month
  • Plus: £300 initial + £50/month
  • Stocks: £1,000 initial + £150/month

🧾 Will I be taxed?

Yes, depending on the plan and profit:

  • Basic: No tax
  • Plus: 10% on profit over £12,000
  • Stocks: 10% over £12,000, 20% over £40,000
